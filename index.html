Everything is possible for man,On the other hand, it may be personally useful for you to assume that things you've never tried are possible, at least until you've tried them and have evidence. And it's good to remember that there's a whole range of 'possible' in between never-tried and professional-level. For example, I'm 68 and have never learned to play a musical instrument. I'm pretty sure that I can't possibly become a concert pianist. I'm also pretty sure that I could learn enough to play simple songs on a piano. What level of piano skill "is possible" for me? Only by trying could I find out.
